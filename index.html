About 01

Engineer who builds end to end

I'm a Data Analytics Engineer with 5 years of experience designing and building data platforms across diverse industries. My work spans the full stack — from streaming ingestion to executive BI dashboards.

I specialize in maintaining, enhancing, and building pipelines — keeping existing solutions healthy, fixing failures fast, and continuously integrating new data sources into the lakehouse as business needs evolve.
